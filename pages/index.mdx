# Introduction
 
Welcome to Career Connections at Notre Dame

## What is this?
Welcome to Career Connections for the Computer Science Department at Notre Dame! This platform is exclusively designed for undergraduates to learn from seniors and graduates about their career paths. Discover the classes they took to achieve their dream jobs and gain insights into the professional journey within the field of computer science. Join us as we bridge the gap between learning and application, empowering you to make informed decisions about your future in the world of computing.
